About Us


OceanDrive Supermarket.A brand built firmly and always destined for success on the big stage.
This top-tier organisation was founded in 2004 by Mister Reagan Ashubwe while he was pursuing his
studies in Mount Kenya University.



His Idea was basically to enable residents of Mount Kenya University and its surroundings to
have access to purchase products that would othewise be found exclusively in towns and big cities
and a cheaper price.


Growing Up and also during his teenage years,Reagan had a deep passion for some hobbies particularly;

  • Playing football.He consequently plays for AC Milan in Italy professionally as a midfielder and won two league titles.

  • He also pursued musicand signed to the OVO and 4L record labels cosigned by Drizzy Drake and 21 Savage.He specialises in rap music.



In the university,Reagan first pursued bachelor in Information Technology that took him four years to complete.
He later rejoined university to pursue his Masters in Computer Science which took him six years and three months.



Reagan,current Chief Marketer Griffins Muhavi and the Sales Manager Stephanie Njoki together with the staff have collaboratively increased
